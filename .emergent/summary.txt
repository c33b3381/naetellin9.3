**Key Systems:**
-   **Editor Suite:** Map Editor (F5), Flight Mode (F6), World Builder (F1), Enemy Editor (F3), and Quest Maker (F7).
-   **Core Gameplay:** A persistent world where player position, level, and XP are saved. It includes character management, inventory, and an integrated quest system.
-   **Vendor System:** Players can sell items to vendor NPCs. The buy functionality is not yet implemented.
-   **Enemy System:** Enemies are persistent, have custom names, respawn in real-time after 15 seconds, and use randomized patrol patterns and combat spreading to avoid stacking.
-   **Death & Resurrection:** Players die at 0 HP, becoming a ghost with a grayscale screen filter. They must teleport to a graveyard, run back to their corpse (a visible character model on the ground), and click a Revive dialog to resurrect at 50% health.
-   **Leveling System:** A WoW-style leveling system is in place up to level 20. Players gain XP from killing mobs, and an XP bar is visible on the HUD. Mob nameplates are colored based on difficulty relative to the player's level.
-   **Skill System:** Warrior skills from the trainer have level requirements.
